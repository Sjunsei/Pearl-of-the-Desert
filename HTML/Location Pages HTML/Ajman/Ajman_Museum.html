Ajman Museum

It is an exhibit that showcases Ajman’s Past from weaponry to old manuscripts, it holds models of dhows and irrigation systems used in the past as well as old pottery and jewelry used for funeral offerings dating back to 3000 BC.

It was originally used as a first line of defense, the Ajman Museum, which opened in 1981, is set in an eighteenth-century fort. The Dhow Yard is one of the most active boatbuilding yards in the country. Al Muwayhat was discovered in 1986 and is a major archaeological site on the outskirts of the city of Ajman.
