National Museum of RAK

The National Museum of Ras Al Khaimah is located In the western part of Ras Al Khaimah city in a fort that was the residence of the ruling family until the early 1960s. The National Museum houses a collection of archaeological and ethnological artifacts.

While its foundations and lower parts originate from 1809-1819, all further additions took place after the peace treaty was signed with the British in 1820.
