Al Biyah Mosque in Fujairah

This historical edifice is an important page out of the region’s cultural and architectural history. The ancient Al-Bidyah Mosque has an exterior built out of stones and burnt mud.

The mosque has four domes supported by a pillar in the middle of the structure. A mihrab and a small minbar (pulpit) with internal openings made for ventilation in an ingenious work of old-school engineering.
