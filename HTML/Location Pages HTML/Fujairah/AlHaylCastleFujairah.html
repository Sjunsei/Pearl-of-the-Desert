Al Hayl Castle Fujairah

When visiting Al Hayl Castle in Fujairah, you’ll get a glimpse of the daily life of the inhabitants of the past. The residential palace compound has meeting rooms known as majlis, storage areas, bedrooms and a kitchen, which visitors can explore.

Don’t forget to visit the meeting room that is attached to the tower in the compound! This majlis boasts beautiful decorative niches, and in the past, would have been decked with rich carpets, lamps, pictures and memorabilia. Outside the compound, you’ll find another informal and open majlis, where guests would drink coffee.
