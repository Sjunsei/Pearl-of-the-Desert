Sheikh Zayed Mosque Fujairah

The Sheikh Zayed Mosque in Fujairah was constructed from the ground up in a span of 700 days, resulting in a place of worship that personifies elegance and grandeur. It involved the hard work and labour of around 600 workers. The design embodies Arabic and Ottoman architecture, along with modern design elements.
