Al Noor Mosque

The word Noor in Arabic means ‘Light’. It was built and ordered by the wife of the ruler of Sharjah, as her Highness Sheikha Jawaher bint Mohammed Al Qassimi, as it was completed in the year 2005. The Exterior of the mosque is complemented with ornaments which are repeated of a floral cornice around the edges of the roof, and the arched doorways and windows. As for the Interior designs the domes are being highly enriched with mixtures of floral, geometrical and other interlaced patterns for non-muslim visitors can visit the most for the guide tour which informs about Islam & the cultural aspects of the life in the UAE.
