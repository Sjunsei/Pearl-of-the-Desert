Sharjah Museum Of Islamic Civilization

In the museum it displays thousands of unique artifacts which highlight the great Islamic Civilization from the first century AH (7AD) to the fourteenth century AH (20 AD). It was first inaugurated in the Heritage Area in the year of 1996 November 6th. The collection of the Museum are the Islamic arts and culture that have exceeded around five thousands definite pieces that are collected from various parts of the Islamic World as for which they are being displayed one their own distinct time periods.
