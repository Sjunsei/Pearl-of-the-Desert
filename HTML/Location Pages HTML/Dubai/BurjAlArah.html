Burj Al Arab

There are 17 different types of pillow to choose from on the “pillow menu”, each catered to the specified needs of each guest.
Burj Al Arab is the only skyscraper in the world that was built on an artificial island 280m (920ft) away from Jumeirah beach. This was built there specifically to avoid having the shadow of the building blocking the beach.
The hotel has 2 big restaurants, one located near the roof of the entire hotel, the other located submerged and underwater underneath the hotel.
