Burj Khalifa

The amount of approximately used resources required to build this would be about 110,000 tons of concrete, 55,000 tons of steel rebar, and about 22 million man-hours just to complete it for an extremely tall and complex building, the Burj Khalifa surprisingly only took about 6 years to finish its completion (2004-2010).
The total amount of weight in concrete used for the building is equal to about 100,000 elephants and the total amount of aluminum that was used is equivalent to 5 A380 aircrafts.
