Formula Rossa

Is an Intamin launch roller coaster that is located at Ferrari World in Abu Dhabi. As it was initially set to open in the year 2010 October 28 however it was deployed until 2010 November 4.

The Formula Rossa is stated to be the fastest coaster in the world at the speed of 149.1 mph (240 KPH). Due to the fact that it’s the fastest ride in the world; customers are regulated to wear protective glasses due to the high accelerations and the occurrence of sand storms in Abu Dhabi.
