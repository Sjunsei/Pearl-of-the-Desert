Qasr Al Hosan

Is one of the oldest and most significant buildings in Abu Dhabi, as it holds to be the first permanent structural city. As for the watchtower it was built around the 1790s, as which the commanding structure is to overlook through the coastal trading routes and protect the growth settlements which establish on the island.
