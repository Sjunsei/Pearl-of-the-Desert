Louvre

The establishment of the louvre hotel was approved by the French Parliament on October 9th 2007. As for the architect for the building is Jean Nouvel and the engineers from BuroHappold Engineering.

The construction was officially started on May 26th 2009 the Crown Prince of Abu Dhabi and the President of France had inaugurated an exhibition titled, Talking Art: Louvre Abu Dhabi at the Gallery One of the Emirates Palace that includes 19 works of art bought over the last 18 months.
