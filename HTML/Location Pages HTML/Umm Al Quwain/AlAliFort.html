Al Ali Fort

It is home to artefacts that were unearthed during Ed-Dur (Ed-Dour) which is one of the largest archaeological dig sites in the UAE and is listed among the most important lost cities of Arabia.

Also known as the Al Ali Fort and was established in 1768 for the current ruling family. It was built on behalf of Sheikh Rashid bin Majid Al Mualla who was the founder of the Al Mualla ruling dynasty.
