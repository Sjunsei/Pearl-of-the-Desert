Pearls of Ajman

Ajman Musuem

Ajman Museum is the emirate's foremost museum illustrating life as it once was. The 18th-century fort served as the ruler's residence until 1970, before it was converted into a museum a decade later. You'll find the museum on the east side of the central square in the Al Bustan area.

Al Zorah Natural Reserve

With one million square metres of natural mangrove forest, Al Zorah Nature Reserve is a haven for bird and marine life. It is a vibrant ecosystem made up of mangroves, turquoise lagoons and sandy beaches, which is home to nearly 58 species of bird including pink flamingo, egret and heron.
